How We Build It...

This Azheimer's Prediction Model is based on Convolutional Neural Network, a class of deep neural networks most commonly applied to analyzing visual imagery.

A Convolutional Neural Network (ConvNet/CNN) is a Deep Learning algorithm which can take in an input image, assign importance (learnable weights and biases) to various aspects/objects in the image and be able to differentiate one from the other.

The model is able to predict three different stages of Alzheimer's Disease, namely NonDemented, MildDemented & VeryMildDemented, with an accuracy of 80%.

About The Dataset

The dataset consists of around 5000 MRI-Scan Images of three different stages of Alzheimer's Disease:

-> Non-Demented

-> Mild-Demented

-> Very-Mild-Demented
